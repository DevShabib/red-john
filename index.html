Red John

Unknown Location · Unknown Base of Operations · redacted@unknown.com

An elusive and dangerous serial killer known for his distinctive calling card and psychological manipulation. Responsible for the deaths of numerous victims, including the family of Patrick Jane.

Profile

Known Information

Psychological Profile

Red John is an extremely intelligent and manipulative individual with a deep understanding of human psychology. He leaves a distinctive signature at his crime scenes and appears to take pleasure in taunting law enforcement, particularly Patrick Jane.

His true identity, background, and motivations remain largely unknown, making him one of the most enigmatic serial killers in criminal history.

Notable Acts

The Murder of Patrick Jane's Family

Personal Connection

Red John's most personal crime was the murder of Patrick Jane's wife and daughter. This act created a lifelong obsession in Jane to find and bring Red John to justice.

Series of Murders

Pattern of Behavior

Red John has been connected to numerous murders across California, each marked by his distinctive calling card. The victims are carefully selected and the crimes meticulously planned.

Methods & Characteristics

Signature Elements
  • Leaves a distinctive calling card at crime scenes
